#### **1. The "Ghost in the Machine" Risk (Critique)**

Your `deception.py` is brilliant, but 2026 regulations (like the EU AI Act) now prioritize **"Interpretability."** If Claude blocks an action for "deception," a developer needs more than a score; they need the specific logic path.

#### **2. Performance Bottlenecks**

You have 60+ endpoints and a `main.py` with 2200 lines. On Replit's standard tier, this could cause cold-start latency that kills the "Real-time" feel of an interceptor.

* **Fix:** Since you're using SSE for the dashboard, consider moving the "Heavy Auditing" (`src/auditor.py`) to an asynchronous task queue or using a faster framework like **FastAPI** if you hit a scaling wall.

#### **3. The "Shadow AI" Discovery Gap**

Your architecture assumes users *know* they have agents. In 2026, the biggest enterprise problem is **"Shadow Agents"** (employees using unvetted agents).

* **Feedback:** Your "Self-Hosted" path is your secret weapon here. Marketing it as a "Local Gateway" that discovers rogue API traffic will be your #1 growth driver.
