Part 2: Privacy Policy (PP)
1. Data Minimization & Usage
Audit Logs: We process "Inner Monologue" and "Tool Call" data solely to generate your Governance Audits and enforcement logs.

The "No-Training" Guarantee: We formally represent that your audit logs, private prompts, and injected credentials are never used to train, fine-tune, or improve any AI models, nor are they shared with third-party model providers for such purposes.

2. Zero-Knowledge Identity Vault
Credentials stored in the Identity Vault are encrypted at rest using industry-standard protocols. The Service utilizes "Ephemeral Injection," meaning your keys are never visible to your agent, never stored in plaintext logs, and exist only in memory during the execution of a verified and approved task.

3. Data Retention & Right to Purge
In compliance with global privacy regulations, all Audit Logs are automatically purged from our systems after 30 days unless you explicitly select a long-term compliance storage plan. You may request a total data wipe at any time through your user dashboard.

4. Automated Decision-Making (ADMT) Rights
The Service facilitates your right to opt-out of "Automated Decision-Making." By default, the Service is configured to require Manual Human Approval for high-risk categories unless you affirmatively create a bypass rule.
