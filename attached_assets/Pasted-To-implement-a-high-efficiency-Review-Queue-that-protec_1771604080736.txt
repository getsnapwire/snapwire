### **1. The "Snap-Card" Review Queue UI Design**

The dashboard will feature a **"Pending Fuses"** sidebar. When an action is blocked, it appears as a card with three instant actions:

* **The Layout:** * **Top Bar:** "Snap Reason" (e.g., *Goal Drift Detected* or *Schema Violation*).
* **Left Side (The Request):** The raw JSON tool call from the AI.
* **Right Side (The Suggestion):** A "Safe Version" or "Explanation" of the block.
* **Footer (The Buttons):** * `[REJECT]` (Red) - Kills the task.
* `[EDIT & RELEASE]` (Blue) - User tweaks the JSON then sends it.
* `[TRUST TOOL]` (Teal) - Releases the action and auto-whitelists this specific pattern for 24 hours.





> **Legal Logic:** By requiring a button click for "Edit & Release" or "Trust," the legal **Duty of Care** remains with the human operator. Snapwire remains the "Passive Intermediary."
