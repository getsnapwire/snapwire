Welcome to the Agentic Firewall

This dashboard helps you stay in control of what your AI agents do. Think of it as a security guard that checks every action before it happens.

⚖️

Your Constitution

You set the rules. The Constitution tab is where you define what your AI is allowed to do -- like spending limits, file access, and more. You can add, edit, or remove rules anytime.

🔓

Approve or Deny

When an agent tries something that breaks a rule, it gets blocked and sent to you. In the Pending Approvals tab, you decide: let it through or shut it down.

🚀

You're All Set!

Use the Test Firewall tab to try sample actions and see how the firewall responds. Hover over anything for a quick explanation. You're in control.
