Colorado SB24-205 Section 6-1-1703: A deployer has an affirmative defense if they can demonstrate they used reasonable care, including risk assessment, ongoing monitoring, and documented human oversight of AI system outputs. Snapwire's audit bundle provides evidence for all three requirements.
Snapwire is a technical monitoring utility. All blocks, alerts, and signals generated are heuristic and advisory in nature. The final Duty of Care for all agent actions and budgetary releases remains solely with the human operator.
